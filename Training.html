W3Schools has been around since 1998, and a lot of people know about it. W3Schools is one of the best places to learn how to code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and more. They offer lots of tutorials on how to code, and they also provide code to use and play around with. Click here to visit W3Schools.

Khan Academy is also a good place to go for those looking to learn how to code in HTML. Apart from HTML, they also provide tutorials and lessons for other subjects, tailored round subjects in secondary school, so it's definitely worth checking out. Click here to visit Khan Academy.

Codeacademy is also a good place to learn how to code. Like W3Schools, they also provide lessons in HTML, CSS and more. However, Codeacademy is mostly paid, so you may have to spend a little to learn about coding in HTML. That being said, they do have professionals giving these courses, so it is worth having a look at. Click here to visit Codeacademy.

Udemy is another good place to learn about HTML and other types of code. Similar to Khan Academy, they also provide classes and lessons for other subjects. Unlike the others, they provide classes for a variety of classes including video editing, art, and other creative areas. Click here to visit Udemy.
